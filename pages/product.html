We are building an open-source archive of civilian-police interactions. It is our hope that this information can serve as a resource to community organizers looking to combat police brutality, to police departments seeking additional insights into the implementation of their training, and to researchers seeking to understand the situational dynamics that result in negative encounters with police.
The world is a better place when you speak truth to power.
